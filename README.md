📌 Project Overview

This repository focuses on Digital Literacy Awareness, aiming to educate users about safe, responsible, and effective use of digital technologies.

It includes practical content such as:

-> Cybersecurity awareness

-> Phishing attack case studies

-> Social media safety guidelines

-> Responsible digital behavior


🎯 Objectives

-> Promote safe internet practices

-> Create awareness about cyber threats (phishing, scams, etc.)

->Encourage responsible digital citizenship

->Help users understand their digital footprint


⚠️ Case Study: Phishing Attack

This project includes a real-world example of a phishing attack (Google Docs 2017 case), showing how attackers:

-> Impersonate trusted platforms

->Trick users into clicking malicious links

->Gain unauthorized access to accounts


💡 Key Learning:

Phishing attacks succeed mainly due to human trust, not just technical vulnerabilities.

📱 Social Media Safety Checklist

✅ Do’s

. Use strong and unique passwords

.Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)

.Verify information before sharing

.Keep your privacy settings updated

.Think before posting online


❌ Don’ts

. Don’t share personal or sensitive information

. Don’t click on suspicious links

. Don’t accept unknown requests

. Don’t overshare your location

. Don’t trust everything online
